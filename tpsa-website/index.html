THEORETICAL PHYSICS STUDENT ASSOCIATION OF IRELAND

Welcome to Ireland's entirely student-run community of Theoretical Physics students and enthusiasts.






Vision


Knowledge shared is knowledge squared

Our goal is to cultivate the interest students have in maths and physics, among other topics, into a lifelongpassion for learning about and bettering our understanding of the world we live in. From weekly seminars to talksgiven by experts in the subject, young minds are inspired and motivated to pursue a stimulating and rewardingcareer in a field they love. A love found, nourished and shared at TPSA.



History


TPSA was founded by Michael Mitchell along with a number of other students in 2019.
While studying Theoretical Physics in TCD, these high-fliers felt there was a gap in
their overall development as up and coming professionals. They created a space where
students could work together, cultivating their social, problem solving and explanatory skills.

They also felt they could lend a hand in putting Ireland, on the map of physics excellence.



Weekly Seminars


We hold seminars every Tuesday evening from 6pm-8pm in the Synge and the Salmon, Hamilton Building, TCD.
The aim here is give like-minded students the opportunity to learn to problem solve together, to receive answers to their questions and to build lasting friendships. Everyone is welcome, you don't have to be a TP student!



Outreach


Bringing our love for maths and physics to younger students, as well as those outside the field, in an attempt to
inspire, motivate and excite has always been a priority at TPSA. We believe in the importance of leadership
and in our responsibility to guide future generations of Ireland so that they can discover the right path for them,
whether that be in STEM or elsewhere.
